Upgrade Policy

As a registered customer, you are entitled to free updates during 18 months from the date of purchase. It means that for 18 months we will provide you with bug fixing and minor feature improvement releases (updates) for your licensed copy of PE Explorer free of charge. You can download and install the latest version of the software from our site, and the license key that you received from us when you purchased the software will work with the latest product version. Just go to the download page and get the latest release.

If you purchased the software more than 18 months ago, you are no longer entitled to free updates. However, you can purchase an upgrade to the latest version at a discounted price, and this upgrade will allow you to have free updates for another 18 months.

If you don't want to purchase an upgrade, you can use the program forever. It will never expire, but you won't be able to use the latest version.
